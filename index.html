The Phased Launch Roadmap

A disciplined, three-stage approach to market entry will manage risk, perfect the operational playbook, and ensure sustainable, long-term growth.

YEAR 1

Stage 1: The Proving Ground

Pilot launch in a select micro-market (e.g., Bengaluru's Koramangala/Indiranagar cluster). The goal is to achieve product-market fit, perfect the operational playbook, and build initial credibility through hyper-local marketing and partnerships.

YEARS 2-3

Stage 2: The Playbook

Expand to cover the entire pilot city and launch in a second Tier-1 city (e.g., Mumbai). The focus shifts to scaling operations, capturing regional market share, and proving the model's replicability.

YEAR 4+

Stage 3: The National Rollout

Strategic expansion to remaining Tier-1 cities (Delhi, Hyderabad) and opportunistic entry into high-growth Tier-2 cities. The goal is national brand leadership and exploring service diversification (grooming, pet-sitting).
